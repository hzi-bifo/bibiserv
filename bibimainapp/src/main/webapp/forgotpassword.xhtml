Password Assistance

Enter your account id associated with your BiBiServ account. We will send you a link to a page where you can enter a new password. If you forgot your account id you have to contact bibi-help@cebitec.uni-bielefeld.de to restore access to your account. Attention: The forgot password form uses the ReCaptcha service to distinguish between computer and human users. To activate ReCaptcha search the web.xml and uncomment then "primefaces.ReCaptcha" context-param entries and add your private/public ReCaptcha key. Check you mail.

If you've entered a valid BiBiserv account, you will receive an mail to your associated mail address from us with instructions for resetting your password. If you don't get this mail please check you junk mail folder or contact us for further help.
